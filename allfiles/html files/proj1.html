Childhood

			Growing up I have always loved the outdoors.  This being the case I choose at a very young age I was going to be a boyscout.  I loved every minute of being a scout and eventually succeeded in becoming an eaglescout(the highest rank).  Although this does pretty much sum up the entirety of my childhood because I spent every other weekend in the wilderness.  I should mention the best memory I have from my past is the two weeks I spent in New Mexico.
